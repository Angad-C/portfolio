Twenty One Pilots Logo Necklace

A custom-machined aluminum necklace created as a personal gift, combining digital design with hands-on fabrication.

The Project

I created a Twenty One Pilots Logo Necklace for my sister who is a huge fan of the band. It was a fun little project for me which allowed me to put some of my skills to use. I used a combination of digital and manual tools to craft it out of a solid block of Aluminum.

Tools & Technologies Used:

Onshape (CAD) Fusion 360 (CAM) CNC Mill Manual Mill
