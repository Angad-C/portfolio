Aviation & Piloting

I'm fascinated by flight - and not just theoretically; I am an FAA-certified (Part 107) commercial drone pilot and dedicated the summer of 2025 to pursuing my private pilot license at the Livermore Airport in California. Learning to fly has been my childhood dream but I did not appreciate how demanding it can be! The flying lessons, and studying for the PPL written exams gave me a much deeper appreciation for airplane design, ground communication, ATC, and airplane pilots.
