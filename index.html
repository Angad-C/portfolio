About Me

I am a student at Olin College of Engineering in Needham, Massachusetts, deeply fascinated by Physics, Astronomy, and the intricate workings of machines. My journey has led me to explore computer programming, 3D modeling, and even aerial photography.

From volunteering at the Chabot Space and Science Center to interning with the Mars Institute and NASA Ames, I'm driven by a desire to contribute to pioneering projects that push the boundaries of exploration and technology. I thrive in collaborative environments where I can apply my skills to create impactful solutions.
